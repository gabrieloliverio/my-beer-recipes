# 25A - Session Belgian Blond Ale

ABV:    4.1%
OG:     1.044
FG:     1.013
EBC:    11.2
IBU:    19
BU/GU:  0.43

# Grist:

Pilsner Malt - 78.5%
Flaked Oats - 20.9%
Black Malt - 0.5%

# Hops

Herkules (14.5% alpha-acid) at 60'

# Yeast

Fermentis T-058 - 1pkg

# Mash profile

65ºC for 60'
Mash-out for 10'

# Fermentation profile:

Primary at 20ºC for 5 days
Secondary at 22ºC for 2 days
Cold crash for 3 days

# Carbonation:

Priming of 5 g/l
