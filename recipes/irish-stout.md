# 15B - Irish Stout

- ABV:    4.7%
- OG:     1.048
- FG:     1.012
- EBC:    86
- IBU:    36
- BU/GU:  0.75

# Grist:

- Pilsner Malt - 62%
- Flaked Oat - 23.8%
- Roasted Barley - 14.2%

# Hops

Admiral (14.8% alpha-acid) - 0.83g/l at 50'

# Yeast

SafAle S-04 - 1pkg

# Mash profile

- 65ºC for 60'
- Mash-out for 10'

# Fermentation profile:

- Primary at 20ºC for 7 days
- Secondary at 22ºC for 2 days
- Cold crash at 2ºC for 5 days

# Carbonation:

Priming of 4 g/l
